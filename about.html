About Us

What the company does

The company provides multiple services which includes abnormal load escorts, abnormal load transport and other forms of transport including multi-axles, abnormal equipment, abnormal load permits, Plant hire services and 32ton flat decks. The company services different sectors from mining companies, transport companies and the government sector.


Strategy

Vision statement

The companies’ vision is to be the favoured leader in the transport industry providing an excellent and timeous service to all clients and an environment where quality services and affordable logistics are key.



Mission statement

LLMP Logistics thrives on being proactive, to always begin with the end in mind, to be innovative adding value to our clients and be excellent in what we do through constant learning and thirst for knowledge.



Our Business Approach

The business approach adopted is underpinned by a definitive strive to gain thorough knowledge of clients’ needs in order to maximize benefits and to add value within the business relationship. A proper assessment of client’s needs by LLMP Logistics will always be made to facilitate client satisfaction at its highest level whilst at the same time, the profits and sustainability of the company are maximized. The company works in conjunction with other larger transport companies to completely satisfy clients’ needs.



Business concept

Business competitiveness

The company provides the highest quality, efficiently and professionally at a reasonable cost. We have formed strategic partnerships with larger and more experienced transport companies to ensure that loads and delivered to clients in a safe and timeous manner. Being a black youth owned company also brings an innovative and technological advancement to our services with reliable drivers and roadworthy vehicles on all jobs.
